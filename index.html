How to Make the Perfect Matcha Latte

Want to know how to make matcha latte at home?

My recipe and tips on making the best green drink you have ever had!

Tools

  • Matcha
  • Matcha Bowl with a Spout
  • Matcha Whisk, Bamboo Scoop, Mesh Strainer
  • Gooseneck Electric Kettle
  • Milk Frother
  • Glass Mug

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cup milk (any kind you prefer)
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon matcha or 1 1/2 matcha bamboo scoops + more for dusting
  • optional: honey or sugar

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together water and sifted matcha powder until all clumps are gone
  2. Heat (don't boil) then froth milk. Put milk in a mug and then pour in matcha tea
  3. Add honey or sugar to sweeten then use a mesh strainer to add a dusting of matcha powder on top
